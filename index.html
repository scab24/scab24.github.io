About

Background

Security researcher and protocol developer specializing in smart contract security, tooling development, and blockchain protocol engineering across EVM and Non-EVM ecosystems. Conducted 25+ private security audits, discovering over 170 vulnerabilities across EVM and Non-EVM protocols.

Built three security tools for Solana at Inversive Labs: Eloizer (static analyzer), Solaris (coverage-guided fuzzer) and Radiant (concolic execution engine).
